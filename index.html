Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients: 1/2 cup milk of your choice, 1/2 frozen banana, 5 frozen strawberries, 1/2 cup frozen blueberries.

Steps: Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth with no chunks.

Toastie

Ingredients: Cheese, bread, deli meat of choice, leafy greens (optional).

Steps: Butter both sides of two slices of bread. Make two sandwiches with ham and cheese in each. Place in a frying pan and heat gently. Once cheese melts and both sides are golden brown, remove, slice, and serve immediately.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients: 1 cup flour, 1 egg, 1 cup milk, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder.

Steps: Mix all ingredients, pour onto a hot pan, cook until golden, flip, and serve with syrup.

Quick Salad

Ingredients: Lettuce, cucumber, tomato, olive oil, salt, pepper.

Steps: Chop veggies, mix in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, season, and enjoy fresh.
